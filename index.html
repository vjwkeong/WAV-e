Who We Are

WAV-E (We Are Volunteers for Empowerment) is a ground-up volunteer initiative led by a network of professionals, technologists, and educators. Our mission is to protect and empower vulnerable communities — especially seniors and low-income groups — in the face of rapidly evolving digital risks.

🎯 Our Mission

Providing free, hands-on workshops that teach foundational knowledge about AI and equip participants with practical anti-scam defence skills. We address the twin gap of AI understanding and scam awareness with dignity, clarity, and empathy.

🧠 The Problem

AI scams are already here: voice cloning impersonates family members, deepfake videos weaponize phishing schemes, and elderly victims are targeted because they're unfamiliar with how advanced and convincing these tools have become.

🔒 Our Philosophy

Prevention is empowerment. We don't just teach seniors how to use AI tools — we arm them with the confidence to say no. WAV-E is about turning fear into familiarity, and technology into trust.
